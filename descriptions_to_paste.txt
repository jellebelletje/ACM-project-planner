In the diagnostic was a resistance risk profile. This needs to be operationalized. For each resistance cluster identified, design a specific intervention and be precise about the mechanism. 
Example: If resistance is rooted in competence, anxiety (people fear looking incompetent withg the new tool)  Intervention is safe practise environments with psychological safety. Not more communications about how great the feature is. 
Example 2: If resistance is structural, for example the change is generally making someone's job harder without a compensating benefit, the intervention might be escalating that finding to governance as a design problem rather than an adoption problem. The resistance management approach should:
1. Name the pattern
2. Attribute the cause
3. Specify the intervention
4. Assign ownership
